Services

Home Services

Insurance Law

“We assist with cases involving denied claims, disputes over policy coverage, and inadequate compensation. Our lawyers are well-versed in insurance regulations and work tirelessly to ensure that our clients receive the compensation they rightfully deserve as well as guiding clients. ”

Banking and Financial Law

“We help individuals navigate issues related to delayed compensation for lost funds, unauthorized charges, and unfair banking practices. We also assist with recovering misappropriated funds and holding banks accountable for their actions. ”

Professional negligence

“We help you pursue compensation for damages and hold the negligent party accountable. Our lawyers understand the Complexities of professional negligence claims and guide you through the legal process effectively. Cases of professional negligence have serious consequences for individuals and their families. ”

Medical Malpractice

“Navigating the complexities of medical malpractice claims requires specialized knowledge and experience. Nawiri Justice's legal team assists you in seeking compensation for injuries and losses caused by medical negligence. We work tirelessly to ensure you receive the justice you deserve. ”

Chama Registration

“Self-help groups commonly referred to as ‘chamas’ are the prevalent saving means in Kenya today.Our lawyers are well equipped to handle chama registration at the comfort of your home.”

 Family Law, Child Adoption and Child Support/Maintenance

“ we have skilled team engaged in the field of family law to take on the role of a negotiator and litigator. Our family law lawyers cover a broad array of domestic issues including: Alimony (Spousal Support), Child Custody Arrangements, Child Support, Divorce and Legal Separation, Divorce, Domestic Violence, Name Change, Prenuptial, Post nuptial and Cohabitation Agreements as well as Adoptions. ”
